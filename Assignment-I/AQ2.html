    Note:
  1. Research Evaluation Committee (REC) will evaluate the publications and assign scores according to the respective journal category.
  2. The grant proposals will be evaluated anonymously by atleast one indepepdent expert.
  3. The grant Proposal evaluation weightage will be 15%, 70%, and 15% for publications (A), independence review (B), and proposal presentation (C) respectively.
  4. The proposals shall secure threshold score of 50%(35 out of 70) in independent review (B) to be eligible for further processing.
  5. Independent reviewer shall provide substantial justification where the proposal scores below 50% and above 90% REC can appoint second independent reviewer where necessary if the evaluation of the first semester examiner seems unsatisfactory.
  6. Publications published within the last five years will be considered for the evaluation purpose (Example: for the grants of 2023, publication from januarat 2019 to 2023 will be considered).
  7. Applicants securing passing score of 50% in aggregate will be qualified (Q) to be considered to receive the grant. Applicants obtaining total scores below 50% will be disqualified (DQ) and rejected.
  8. Applicant obtaining the passing scores will be ranked on the basis of the total scores and the required number of grantees will be selected on the basis of the order of merit.
