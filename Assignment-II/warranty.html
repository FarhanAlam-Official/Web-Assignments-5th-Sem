Warranty & Services

At Luxury Watches, we pride ourselves on the quality and craftsmanship of our timepieces. Our Warranty and Services section ensures a seamless experience for our customers.

Warranty Coverage

  • All watches come with a 3-year limited warranty.
  • Covers manufacturing defects such as movement issues, case imperfections, and battery replacement.
  • Does not cover normal wear and tear, water damage, or mishandling.

To claim a warranty, please provide proof of purchase and the original warranty card.

Return Policy

Our return policy is simple and hassle-free:

  • Returns accepted within 30 days of purchase.
  • Items must be in their original packaging and unused.
  • Shipping costs for returns are the responsibility of the customer unless the product is defective.

Contact our customer support team to initiate a return.
